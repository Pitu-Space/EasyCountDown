Privacy Policy

Easy CountDown 2

Last updated: May 2026

1. Overview

Easy CountDown 2 (“the App”) helps you track deadlines on your device. This policy describes how information is handled when you use the App. The developer does not operate a backend server for the App’s core features described below.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, store on our servers, or ask you to provide:

  • An account, login, or password
  • Your name, email, or phone number (unless you email us voluntarily)
  • Precise location or contacts
  • Advertising identifiers for third-party ad networks

There is no sign-up and no user database operated by the developer for routine use of the App.

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Deadline information you enter (such as titles, notes, dates, and importance) is stored only on your iPhone using Apple’s SwiftData storage. It remains under your control and is not sent to the developer’s servers.

To support the Home Screen widget, a small amount of data (including which deadline you choose to pin) may be stored in shared on-device storage (App Group) so the main app and the widget extension can read it. This stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.

Deleting the App removes the App’s local data from your device, subject to how iOS handles removal.

4. In-App Purchases

The App does not currently offer in-app purchases. If optional purchases are added in a future version, payment will be processed by Apple; the developer does not receive or store your card details.

5. Apple Analytics and Crash Reporting

Apple may automatically collect analytics such as installation counts, crash reports, and performance metrics. This information is collected and processed by Apple, not by the developer.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal data from children for marketing purposes.

7. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate third-party analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or external data-collection services in the current version. If that changes in the future, this policy will be updated.

8. Future Features (e.g. Cloud Sync)

If cloud sync or similar features are added later, they may rely on Apple services (such as iCloud) and would be described in an updated policy and in App Store privacy details. Until then, your deadlines remain stored locally as described above.

9. GDPR, CCPA, and Similar Laws

Because the App does not send your deadline content to the developer’s servers for routine operation, there is nothing for us to export or delete on our systems—you may remove data by editing or deleting events in the App, or by deleting the App. If you contact us, we only process the information in your message for support.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. Updated versions will show a new “Last updated” date at the top.
