Easy CountDown!

Welcome to the support page for Easy CountDown 2. Track deadlines with clarity—on your calendar and on your Home Screen.

What the app does

Add upcoming deadlines and browse past ones. Countdowns use years or days when you’re far out, and switch to hours inside the last 24 hours. The calendar overview shows how busy your months are, with importance color-coding so priorities stand out. Your data stays on your iPhone with SwiftData—no account required.

Home Screen widget

Pin one deadline to the widget—upcoming or past— for a clean countdown at a glance. Tap the widget to jump back into the app when you need the full picture.
